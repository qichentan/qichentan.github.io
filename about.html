My Profile 关于我

TylerTam was born in Guangdong, China. He is a PhD student in The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST). Before, he obtained bachelor degree from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC). His research interest is about urban aerial mobility acoustic and urban low-noise planning, and he is also engaged in software system development.

TylerTam出生于中国广东省。他现在是香港科技大学（HKUST）的一名博士生。再此之前，他获得了电子科技大学（UESTC）的工学学士学位。他的研究兴趣是城市空中交通声学和城市低噪声规划，同时从事软件系统开发工作。

He is fascinated on music, which has been a indispensable part of his life. At the age of eight, he learnt piano from Mr. Wentao Li, who cultivates his solid basic skills and interest on music. Meanwhile, he also took solfeggio courses from 2008 to 2012. In 2011, he won the Amateur Ten Exam with the only "Good" level in the whole district. During 2009-2012, he participated in several provincial piano competitions and won awards. After 2012, although he paused studying piano, his love for music has not diminished. From 2016 to 2020, he served as the piano accompaniment for the choir of UESTC. During the college life, he played and sang with friends who love music. In 2018, he composed his first original music. Since then, he starts his songwriting career in the busy gap...

TylerTam对音乐着迷，音乐已成为他生活中不可缺少的一部分。他自八岁起随李文涛老师学习钢琴,练就了扎实的基本功的同时也培养了对音乐的兴趣。同时，他还在2008年至2012年期间修习视唱练耳课程。2011年，他以全区唯一的“良好”等级获得中央音乐学院业余十级证书。 2009-2012年期间，他 多次参加省级钢琴比赛并获奖。 2012年后，他虽然没有继续学习钢琴，但对音乐的热爱并没有消减。 2016年至2020年担任电子科技大学合唱团钢琴伴奏。大学期间，他还和众多热爱音乐的朋友一起弹奏、唱歌。 2018年，他创作了他的第一首原创音乐《夏日遐想》。从此，他在忙碌的工作之余开始了自己的创作生涯……
